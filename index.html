About The App

News Zone is a simple application with news articles generated from an API (newsapi.org). The app has capability to search through top headlines, sort by category and sort by country of origin the news article has come from. The task was to use an API and get data and make a simple app that shows the data with capability to do simple searching.

In the world of technology mobile news sites are being used more than print media. This app created by Holliday Robbins simply shows the articles from external sources along with photos and headlines.
